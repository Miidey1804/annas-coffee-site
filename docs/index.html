Our Story

Rooted in legacy. Roasted with love.

At Anna’s Coffee, our journey begins where the story of coffee itself began—in the lush, misty highlands of Kaffa, southwestern Ethiopia, the ancestral home of Coffea arabica. This sacred origin inspires everything we do, from how we source our beans to how we serve our community.

We proudly source our flagship beans from these ancient forests—where coffee still grows wild. Our farmers, using traditional methods passed down through generations, cultivate beans with rich, complex character.

From bean to cup, we embrace sustainability, fair trade, and artisan roasting. Anna’s Coffee isn’t just a drink—it’s a tribute to heritage, craftsmanship, and conscious living.

More than coffee—it's a cultural legacy in every cup.
